Delivering Career and Learning Outcomes


Find Out More Log In

ClaroNex is the education platform that helps people reach their career and learning goals faster and easier.

We partner with employers, learning organizations and educational content owners to benchmark outcomes, and then deliver using adaptive learning and smart content.

Our core objectives are to:

1) Uplift science, technology, engineering and math learning outcomes in schools

2) Map clear pathways for careers and learning through higher, vocational and professional education



Value

- Build personalized, multi-year relationships with learners

- Create new revenue opportunities

- Uplift the value of learning materials

- Attract and retain more learners with improved pass rates (10%+) and higher engagement (course completion rates 10%+)

- Optimize content (20%+ saving)

- Benefit from fast delivery and an 80% saving versus building custom solutions

Approach

Our modular platform and end-to-end capability allow us to quickly implement your solution. We remove the triple challenge of co-ordinating and implementing (1) a market leading learner outcomes framework at the same time as (2) delivering personalized learning pathways and (3) revamping content strategy.

ClaroNex is a flexible, modular approach to design and delivery of optimized career and learning. The ClaroNex platform delivers personalized pathways and outcomes to learners. We work alongside employers, learning organizations and educational content owners to enable them with 21st century learning solutions. ClaroNex integrates easily with existing processes with minimum effort and maximum effect.




How we help clients

Employer

Pinpoint organization needs and skills requirements. Align with career goals and skills gaps to drive career and learning pathways. Deliver using a personalized approach and the best mix of internal and third party content.

K-12 and Higher Education

Define clear career pathways and benchmark learner outcomes, profile learners and deliver an integrated learning experience using optimized educational content.

Skills to Work Programme

Benchmark programme objectives and skills requirements. Align with participant career goals and skills gaps to drive career and learning pathways. Deliver using an integrated approach and optimized educational content.

Content Provider

Define and benchmark learner outcomes, provide integrated learning experience built on existing and new content libraries.
