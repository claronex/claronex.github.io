About us

About us

We are investors and new venture developments. We target projects that are global, scaleable and sustainable. Claronex aims to generate large socio-economic benefits with attractive financial returns.

Core sectors:

Energy

Infrastructure

Water

Claronex partners with clients to unlock value from new opportunities and existing assets. We lead financing rounds, structuring and implementation of projects. We then deliver by partnering with specialists to fast track design, delivery and operation.

Strategic outlook

We are proactively seeking opportunities across EMEA and South Asia that:

  • Increase the availability of resources, energy and water.
  • Improve the efficiency of infrastructure, energy and water use.
  • Accelerate the implementation of leading edge technology to lower implementation and operation costs.
  • Bring together development programmes and innovation financing initiatives to co-develop new opportunities.

Our team and partner network have skills spanning strategy, finance, programme management, technology implementation and business transformation. We have a strong heritage of delivering projects with complex value chains across numerous industries.
