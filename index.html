Business Incubator and Growth Accelerator



ClaroNex is a business incubator and growth accelerator specialized in the media, education, communications and technology sectors. We partner with start-ups and growth companies to drive opportunities from idea to realization.

Our incubator is focused on developing early stage businesses, our growth accelerator enables established businesses to deliver growth through new services and in new territories. We are based in London, but extend to a global reach through our partner network.


Business Incubator


The ClaroNex incubator is a launch pad for early stage businesses. We enable innovative businesses on the path from concept to growth. Our incubator helps entrepreneurs solve the challenges in the start-up phase of new ventures. These can range from evolving proof of concepts, generating early sales, fund raising and setting up offices.


Each new opportunity and existing capabilities are different, so our approach is tailored to business needs. We offer the following services to enable new businesses to succeed:

  • Strategic planning and roadmap development

  • Funding and fund raising

  • Subject matter experts

  • Sales specialists

  • Global reach

  • Great offices and meeting space



Growth Accelerator


Our growth accelerator is focused on taking businesses to the next level. We enable businesses to accelerate their growth into new territories and into new products and services. ClaroNex works closely with our clients to de-risk expansion activities, accelerate launch success and optimize cash flows.


Claronex has a network of specialists and international reach with deep experience in strategy, corporate finance, sales and operations. Our accelerator works with businesses to precise requirements, formulate plans and then work with clients to put these plans into action. This is done through an optimal mix of investment in organic growth, partnerships and M&A.


Our services include:

  • Strategic market entry planning

  • Growth finance fund raising

  • M&A and partnership development

  • Sales and marketing - strategy, forecast and delivery

  • Technology extension and localization

  • Regulatory compliance

  • Local management, offices and administration
