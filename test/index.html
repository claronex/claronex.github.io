Welcome to the Claronex Parks

Claronex Parks is an exciting new project in the East of United Kingdom. Set across 25 acres and situated 20 minutes from the airport, Claronex Parks brings together the benefits of clean air, clean water and it’s own integrated solar power generation and management system. This stylish, safe and secure gated community is designed around open park spaces with shops and leisure facilities. Claronex Parks combines the benefits of quality, clean and comfortable living with the convenience being close to downtown United Kingdom.
